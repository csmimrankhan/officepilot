# Product Overview (Non-Technical)

## 1. Simple Summary
OfficePilot AI is your safe AI office assistant. It takes the manual work out of processing invoices and managing office data, all while keeping your information private on your own computer.

---

## 2. The Problem
Most businesses spend hours every week:
- Opening invoice emails.
- Typing invoice data into Excel.
- Manually creating drafts in QuickBooks or Xero.
- Fixing data entry mistakes.
- Searching for lost paper trails.

Existing AI tools are often "all or nothing"—they either don't do enough, or they take too much control and risk making mistakes with your money.

---

## 3. The Solution
OfficePilot AI acts as a **controlled digital employee**. It reads your invoices, extracts the numbers, and asks you: *"Is this correct?"*

Once you say yes, it can update your spreadsheets and prepare your accounting software automatically. It never makes a payment or changes a final record without your explicit permission.

---

## 4. Key Benefits
- **Save Time**: Reduce invoice entry time by up to 80%.
- **Eliminate Errors**: AI extraction is more consistent than manual typing.
- **Total Safety**: You approve every action. If something goes wrong, use the "Undo" (Restore) button.
- **Privacy First**: Your data stays on your computer. It doesn't live in the "cloud" unless you choose to use cloud features.
- **Audit Ready**: Every change is tracked. You can prove exactly where every number came from.

---

## 5. What it can do TODAY
- ✅ Read PDF and image invoices.
- ✅ Pull invoices directly from your Gmail or Outlook.
- ✅ Flag duplicate invoices automatically.
- ✅ Export data to beautiful, organized Excel reports.
- ✅ Prepare draft invoices in QuickBooks and Xero.
- ✅ Respond to voice commands like "Show me pending invoices."
- ✅ Record and replay repetitive office tasks.

---

## 6. Our Safety Promise
OfficePilot AI is built on a "Safe-by-Design" philosophy:
- **No Payments**: It cannot move money.
- **No Hidden Actions**: It doesn't click things behind your back.
- **Approval Required**: Every business-critical action requires a human click.
- **Emergency Stop**: One button to freeze all system activity.

---

## 7. Is it right for my business?
OfficePilot is perfect for:
- **Small Businesses**: Who want to automate without hiring more staff.
- **Accountants & Bookkeepers**: Who manage multiple clients and high invoice volumes.
- **Admin Teams**: Who need a better way to track spend and audit trails.

---

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions
**Is my data safe?**
Yes. Unlike many AI tools, OfficePilot stores your data locally. We don't sell your data or use it to train AI models.

**Do I need to be a tech expert?**
No. If you can use a web browser and Excel, you can use OfficePilot.

**What if the AI makes a mistake?**
The system highlights fields it's unsure about. You always have the final word. If you approve an error, you can use the "Restore" feature to undo the change.

**Can it work with my existing Excel files?**
Yes. OfficePilot can add data to your existing spreadsheets without breaking your formulas.
