## Quick Reference

- **One-liner**: "OfficePilot AI turns invoice emails and PDFs into Excel and accounting drafts, with approval, audit logs, and restore."
- **Target users**: Accountants, bookkeepers, admin teams, SME owners, BPO/outsourcing firms
- **Pilot status**: Free during pilot, no credit card, sample data included
- **Tone**: Honest, founder-to-founder, no fake claims, no enterprise exaggeration

## Usage Guide

1. Start with **FOUNDER_PITCH.md** — internalize the one-liner before any outreach
2. Use **PILOT_OUTREACH_MESSAGES.md** for first contact
3. Screen with **PILOT_QUALIFICATION_QUESTIONS.md**
4. Schedule demo calls and follow **PILOT_INTERVIEW_SCRIPT.md**
5. Prepare with **DEMO_CHECKLIST.md**
6. Score with **PILOT_FEEDBACK_SCORECARD.md**
7. Reference **DEMO_VIDEO_3_MIN_SCRIPT.md** and **60_SEC_SCRIPT.md** for recording/website assets
8. Point waitlist signups to /landing.html or /welcome
