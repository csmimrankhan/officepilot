## Scoring Interpretation

| Total | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 20-25 | Hot lead — prioritize for onboarding |
| 15-19 | Warm lead — nurture with follow-up |
| 10-14 | Cool lead — may convert over time |
| 5-9 | Not ready — pass for now |

## Usage Tips

- Fill out the scorecard within 2 hours of the call while details are fresh.
- Score each dimension independently — a low Pain Intensity does not disqualify a high Workflow Fit.
- Annotate scores with specific quotes or observations in the Notes column.
- Log all objections verbatim in the Objections Log; track which responses actually resolve concerns.
- Update the Stage field on every follow-up so the scorecard reflects the full lifecycle.
- Re-score after major milestones (demo, trial start, onboarding completion).
- Share anonymised trends with the product team monthly to identify patterns.
