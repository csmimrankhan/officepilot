# Product Positioning

## What OfficePilot AI Is

### Invoice Automation
- Reads invoice PDFs and emails
- Extracts vendor, total, line items, and dates — all locally
- Supports multiple parser engines (existing, OCR)

### Excel Export & Accounting Draft Sync
- Exports structured Excel files with line items
- Syncs draft entries to QuickBooks Online and Xero
- Preview-first: review before any data leaves the system

### Workflow Recording & Replay
- Record repetitive invoice processing steps
- Replay with dry-run mode for safety
- Step-by-step approval checkpoints

### Browser & Screen Assistant (with Approval)
- Browser automation for fill/submit operations
- Screen context detection with OCR
- Every write/click requires approval

### Audit-Logged & Restore-Safe
- Every action timestamped and attributed
- Version history for invoices, settings, workflows
- File snapshots before every mutation

## What OfficePilot AI Is NOT

### ❌ A General-Purpose Chatbot or AI Assistant
Not a conversational AI. Not a replacement for ChatGPT or Claude.

### ❌ A Screen-Clicking Bot
Not designed to automate arbitrary desktop applications. Screen control is
read-only by default and write operations require explicit opt-in.

### ❌ A Banking or Payment System
Does not connect to banks, process payments, or automate banking websites.
Banking domains are blocked in browser automation mode.

### ❌ A Replacement for Your Accountant
Automates data entry and reduces manual work, but does not make accounting
decisions or provide financial advice.

### ❌ A Cloud Service That Sends Data to External AI
All data stays local. No external AI APIs. No cloud LLMs.

### ❌ A No-Code Automation Platform
Not a general-purpose alternative to Zapier, Make, or Power Automate.
Focused specifically on invoice processing workflows.

## Target Audience

- **Accountants & Bookkeepers** — Automate invoice data entry, focus on exceptions
- **Admin Teams** — Stop copy-pasting between email, Excel, and accounting systems
- **SME Owners** — Get visibility into payables without hiring a full-time AP clerk
- **BPO / Outsourcing Firms** — Standardize invoice processing across multiple clients

## Key Differentiators

- **Local-first** — No cloud dependency, no data leaving your machine
- **Approval gates** — Every write operation requires explicit approval
- **Audit & restore** — Full change history with one-click restore
- **Open source** — Code published for review and self-hosting
